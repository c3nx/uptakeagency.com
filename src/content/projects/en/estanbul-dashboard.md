## Overview

A comprehensive operations and analytics dashboard built for Estanbul's gaming entertainment venues. The platform centralizes venue management, personnel tracking, and business analytics into a single real-time interface, enabling management to make data-driven decisions across all locations.

## Key Features

- **Personnel tracking system**: Employee attendance, clock-in/clock-out, and work hour monitoring across all venues
- **Shift management**: Automated shift scheduling, swap requests, and overtime tracking with conflict detection
- **Real-time venue analytics**: Live data on venue occupancy, revenue per station, and customer traffic patterns
- **Financial reporting**: Daily revenue breakdowns, expense tracking, and profitability analysis per venue and per station
- **Inventory management**: Gaming hardware inventory, maintenance schedules, and replacement tracking
- **Staff performance**: Individual and team performance metrics, productivity analysis, and KPI dashboards
- **Multi-venue support**: Centralized view across all locations with drill-down capabilities

## Personnel Module

The personnel module provides complete HR management for venue operations:

- Biometric attendance integration for accurate time tracking
- Automated payroll hour calculations with overtime rules
- Leave management with approval workflows
- Staff scheduling with shift templates and rotation patterns
- Performance reviews and incident logging

## Impact

Replaced multiple disconnected spreadsheets and manual processes with a unified platform, reducing administrative overhead and providing management with real-time visibility into operations across all venues.
