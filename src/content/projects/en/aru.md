## Overview

ARU is a comprehensive CRM and marketing automation platform built on the WhatsApp Business Platform. It enables small and medium businesses to manage customer relationships, automate communications, and run targeted marketing campaigns.

## Challenge

SMBs in Turkey needed an affordable, easy-to-use platform to manage customer communications via WhatsApp—the dominant messaging platform in the region. Existing solutions were either too complex or lacked WhatsApp integration.

## Solution

We built ARU as a SaaS platform that integrates directly with Meta's WhatsApp Business Cloud API, providing businesses with powerful automation tools through a simple, intuitive interface.

## Key Features

- **Appointment reminders**: Automated WhatsApp reminders 24h and 2h before appointments
- **Campaign management**: Template-based marketing campaigns with audience segmentation
- **AI-powered messaging**: Gemini AI integration for personalized message generation
- **Customer database**: Complete CRM with visit history, purchase records, and contact management
- **Analytics**: Delivery tracking, open rates, and campaign performance metrics
- **Role-based access**: Admin, manager, and staff permission levels

## Impact

- Serving multiple active businesses across Turkey
- Processing thousands of automated messages monthly
- Measurable improvement in customer retention rates for clients
