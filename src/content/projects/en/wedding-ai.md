## Overview

An AI-powered creative platform that uses generative AI to help couples visualize wedding themes, decorations, and venues. Generate realistic previews of different wedding styles and arrangements.

## Key Features

- **AI generation**: Create realistic wedding visualizations from text descriptions
- **Style exploration**: Browse and remix different wedding aesthetics
- **Venue preview**: Visualize how a venue would look with different decorations
- **Shareable**: Export and share visualizations with wedding planners and vendors
