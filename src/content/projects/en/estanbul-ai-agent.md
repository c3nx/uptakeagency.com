## Overview

Estanbul AI Agent is an intelligent multi-channel customer service system built for one of Turkey's leading gaming entertainment companies. The agent autonomously handles customer inquiries across WhatsApp, web chat, and social media platforms.

## Challenge

The client was managing thousands of daily customer interactions manually across multiple channels. Response times were inconsistent, and scaling the support team was becoming cost-prohibitive.

## Solution

We developed an AI-powered agent leveraging large language models with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to provide accurate, context-aware responses. The system integrates with WhatsApp Business API, web chat widgets, and social media APIs to provide a unified customer experience.

## Key Features

- **Multi-channel support**: Unified conversation management across WhatsApp, web, and social media
- **RAG-based responses**: Accurate answers from a curated knowledge base
- **Human handoff**: Seamless escalation to human agents for complex queries
- **Analytics dashboard**: Real-time metrics on conversation volume, resolution rates, and customer satisfaction

## Results

- 70% reduction in average response time
- 85% of inquiries resolved without human intervention
- Consistent 24/7 customer support coverage
