## Overview

Manual, repetitive processes drain your team's time and introduce errors. We design and implement automation systems that connect your tools, route your data, and keep your operations moving without constant human intervention.

## What We Automate

- **Workflow Automation**: Trigger-based pipelines that handle approvals, notifications, and data sync across systems
- **CRM Integration**: Bi-directional sync between your CRM and external platforms — sales, support, marketing
- **WhatsApp Business API**: Programmatic messaging for customer onboarding, transactional alerts, and support flows
- **Multi-Channel Messaging**: Unified communication infrastructure spanning email, SMS, WhatsApp, and web chat

## Integration Approach

We map your existing toolchain first, then design integrations that are idempotent and resilient to failures. Each integration includes logging and alerting so issues surface quickly.

## Results You Can Expect

Less time spent on data entry, fewer dropped hand-offs between teams, and consistent communication with customers at every stage of their journey.
