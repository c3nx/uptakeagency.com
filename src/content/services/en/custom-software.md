## Overview

We build software that fits your processes — not the other way around. From internal tools to customer-facing platforms, we deliver clean, scalable code that your team can own and extend.

## What We Build

- **Web Applications**: Responsive, performant front-ends paired with robust back-end logic
- **APIs & Integrations**: RESTful and GraphQL APIs designed for reliability and developer experience
- **Platforms & Dashboards**: Data-rich interfaces for operations, analytics, and administration
- **Third-Party Integrations**: Connecting your systems to payment gateways, CRMs, ERPs, and communication tools

## Tech Stack

We use modern, proven technologies — TypeScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL — chosen for the right reasons, not trend-following. Infrastructure runs on cloud providers with containerized deployments.

## Delivery

We work in short sprints with regular check-ins. You see working software early and often, with the ability to reprioritize as requirements sharpen.
